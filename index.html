                    How to play

                    Controls

                    • WASD or Arrows — move the detective
                    • E / Space — interact, advance dialogue
                    • Tab / I — toggle the notebook
                    • H or ? — this help
                    • Esc — close any open panel

                    Investigating

                    • A gold ring highlights the nearest object. Walk up until the E prompt appears.
                    • Every clue and statement goes into your notebook. Open it any time with Tab.
                    • If a suspect has said something the evidence contradicts, a red ⚑ Challenge option appears in their dialogue. Pick it, then choose the item that breaks their story.
                    • The Make Accusation button unlocks once you've heard every suspect out, collected enough clues, and caught at least one critical lie. The stronger your case, the better the ending.
                    A mystery in one evening

                    The Last Interview

                    A journalist is dead in Suite 409. You have one shift to walk the Meridian, read the room, and press three suspects until a single unbroken story survives.

                    WASD / Arrows — move
                    E / Space — interact / advance
                    Tab — notebook  ·  H — help  ·  Esc — close
                    10–20 minutes · keyboard + mouse · headphones optional
